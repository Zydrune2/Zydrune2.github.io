Pet Hotel offers a premium daycare service. Bring your pet in to a cozy facility that ensures welcomed stay, safe play, and personal care.

An idea to open Pet Hotel came to me several years ago, once I was walking my boss’s dog Marco. He wasn’t the first dog left with me while their owners where on vacation or unexpected trip. That afternoon by the sea it occurred to me that I should offer my services to other people as well.

Since then I have took care of over 50 pets, both in my house and in clients’. Taking care of pets gives me lots of joy, also lots of exercise… I guarantee one-on-one care for your pet as I take on one pet at the time.
